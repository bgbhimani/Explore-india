About

Rann Utsav is a cultural festival held in the Great Rann of Kutch in Gujarat, India. It is a celebration of the rich cultural heritage of the region, featuring traditional music, dance, crafts, and cuisine. The festival offers a unique opportunity to experience the vibrant culture and stunning landscapes of Kutch.
